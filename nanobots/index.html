How IMMUNE nanobot assistants help you?

IMMUNE nanobot assistants programmed on your needs

Required medical examinations and DNA samples that you provide are engineered on each nanobot assistant to make sure that your body will cooperate with your new IMMUNE system.

IMMUNE nanobot assistants injected in your body

The personalized assistants are entering your body via a series of injections while IMMUNE personell is securing the smooth behaviour of your body.

When this process is over, you are having an additional 200 - 500 IMMUNE nanobot assistants per microliter of blood (mcL).

Assimilation process

When IMMUNE nanobot assistants are in your body there is a period of adaptation that lasts between 1 to 3 weeks.

During this period you might experience some side-effects like fever or mild nausea. IMMUNE personell is available during this period and the side-effects are not severe.

Extensive testing has shown that only a 5% of subjects are showing above minor symptoms.

Your IMMUNE 2.0 at work!

Your new immune system, starts immediately assisting your body needs! It takes action by prioritizing your body needs, so the most severe injuries, malfunctions are first to be treated.

Depending on the treatment focus, IMMUNE nanobot assistants are smartly distributed to achieve maximal healing. For example, treating a mutilated member or an organ regeneration will require all IMMUNE resources and an illness might not be treated simultaneously.

Continuous improvement and learning

IMMUNE nanobot assistants are constantly feeding IMMUNE AI companion with your body metrics, analytics and treatment behaviour. This way, further adjustments will be automatically applied in future treatments.
