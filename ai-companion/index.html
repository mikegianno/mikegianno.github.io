How IMMUNE AI companion works?

IMMUNE AI companion embedded in your body

IMMUNE AI companion is placed just under your skin in the base of your neck. The companion is self-sustained, picking up energy from your body movement.

IMMUNE AI companion activated

IMMUNE AI companion is the first to be activated and tested even before injecting your IMMUNE Nanobot assistants. After activation you will rarely have to interact with IMMUNE AI companion, all is taken care of automatically.

There is an accompanying app available but it's not suggested. Under testing we have seen that interacting with the app increases body stess levels, having a negative overall experience.

A single touch you can control

Despite the fact that IMMUNE AI companion needs no management from your side, there is a safety mechanism for you to manually control the companion. Gently pressing for 10 seconds with your thumb the area where the IMMUNE AI companion is added you can switch it on and off accordingly.

IMMUNE AI assistant is the "mind" of your IMMUNE experience and we constantly strive to make it better, so updates will be often! When an update is available, we will contact you directly with detailed instructions on how to perform the update.
